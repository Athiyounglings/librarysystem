Welcome To The Library

Little Story

Alpha library management system is a system that that allows people to borrow books online making it easier and more convenient for all. You are given easy access to search for books you want and desire in the comfort of your own space without having to manually go to a public library facility, the great part is that if the book is not available then there are options of getting the required book in a different public library. Users are having hard times going to library and it is time wasting going to and forth just to look for a book besides that we are generating new technology just to make it easier and more efficient. With the system in place, we trying to reduce the damage of library books, people not returning books, now will keep tab of the ins and outs of the books.


We saw it fit that people needed change and exposure to Technology. We wanted to change their perspective in doing so we showing them that we need to move with time, to elevate and improve the library system. At that time, we had a lot of leverage because people were concerned about their safety due to the current pandemic, which is still an ongoing factor and libraries were closed while schools commenced that worked to our advantage.
